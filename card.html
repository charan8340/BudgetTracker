Zero-Based Budgeting

Assign Every dollar of income a specific purpose,ensuring expenses align with income.

Envelope System

Allocate cash to Physical envelopes for different spending categories,promoting disciplined spending.

50/30/20 Rule

Divide income into needs, wants and savings/debts ,offering a simple guidelines to balance financial goals.

Pay Yourself First Budget

Priortize Saving by allocating a portion of income to savings and investments.

Value Based - Budgeting

Ensuring Money is spent in alignment with what matters most to you.
